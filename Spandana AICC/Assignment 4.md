# AI Around Me

Description: AI is integrated into many daily tools and services. Machine Learning (ML) helps these applications improve by learning patterns from data, predicting outcomes, or making decisions.

10 Real-Life AI Applications

1. Google Search

AI suggests relevant results instantly.

ML: Algorithms rank pages by learning which results users click most for a query.

2. YouTube Recommendations

Shows videos based on your watch history.

ML: Collaborative filtering predicts what you might like based on patterns from millions of users.

3. Netflix / Streaming Services

Suggests movies and shows.

ML: Analyzes viewing habits, watch time, and ratings to predict your preferences.

4. Smartphone Keyboard (e.g., Gboard, SwiftKey)

Predicts next words or autocorrects.

ML: Uses sequence models to learn your typing patterns and common phrases.

5. Amazon / E-commerce Recommendations

Suggests products when shopping online.

ML: Analyzes purchase history and similar user behavior to recommend items.

6. Navigation Apps (Google Maps, Waze)

Predicts traffic and best routes.

ML: Learns traffic patterns from GPS data and predicts travel times in real time.

7. Spotify / Music Apps

Recommends songs and playlists.

ML: Uses collaborative filtering and audio feature analysis to suggest music you might enjoy.

8. Email Spam Filters (Gmail, Outlook)

Filters spam or phishing emails automatically.

ML: Learns patterns in emails (keywords, sender behavior) to classify spam vs. safe emails.

9. Voice Assistants (Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant)

Understands voice commands and responds.

ML: Speech recognition models convert voice to text and predict appropriate responses.

10. Social Media Feeds (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok)

Shows posts and ads tailored to you.

ML: Analyzes interactions, likes, and watch times to prioritize content you’re most likely to engage with.
